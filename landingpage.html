Camping Essentials

When heading for a camping trip, make sure to bring the following essentials:

  • A sturdy tent and sleeping bag for a comfortable night's sleep.
  • Cooking supplies, including a portable stove and utensils.
  • Weather-appropriate clothing and layers.
  • A reliable flashlight or headlamp for nighttime activities.
  • Insect repellent and sunscreen to stay protected outdoors.
